# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three overlapping audiences, all landing on a single-page portfolio:

- **Hiring managers & recruiters** sourcing senior GTM / revenue leadership. Skimming for proof: numbers, deals, scope. Want fast signal that this person closes and leads.
- **Founders & early-stage teams** evaluating a GTM hire, advisor, or collaborator. Want to see how he thinks and builds, not just what he sold.
- **Peer network** (operators, ex-colleagues) checking in or referring.

Context of use: arriving from LinkedIn, a referral, or a DM, often on mobile, giving the page 30–60 seconds before deciding whether to reach out. Success = they contact him, book a call, or download the CV.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio for Antonello Totaro: Global GTM leader, AI-native builder (creator of Toni CRM, co-designer of the Humanitec Sandbox), Barcelona-based. The page exists to convert credibility into a conversation. It must prove a track record (solo deal wins, partnerships, numbers) while showing range that a résumé can't: that he builds his own tools, has roots and a point of view, and is a distinct human, not a list of bullet points. Currently signalling "open to" new roles.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, warm, proven. Voice is confident without bragging, precise without jargon. Three feelings to land in the first five seconds: **confident/senior** (established operator, real numbers, gravitas), **builder/technical** (hands-on, AI-native, ships product), and **distinctive/human** (personality and warmth via roots, languages, the birth-chart section, not a template). Editorial restraint over hype.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS landing.** No gradient blobs, no hero-metric template, no identical feature-card grids, no Inter-everything.
- **LinkedIn / corporate CV.** No stock-blue, no buzzword soup, no timeline-résumé layout, no headshot-and-badges look.
- **AI-slop dark portfolio.** No neon-on-black, no decorative glassmorphism, no glowing gradient text, no off-the-shelf dev-folio template clone.

Current warm-dark editorial direction (Instrument Serif display, DM Mono labels, near-black tinted neutrals, restrained gold/sand accent) is the right lane. Keep it committed.

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over claims.** Lead with numbers, deals, and shipped things. Show the work; let it carry the bragging.
2. **Build is the brand.** The fact that he builds his own tools (Toni CRM) is differentiation, not a footnote. Surface the maker.
3. **Human, not a template.** Roots, languages, and the birth chart make the page unmistakably his. Protect the personality; never sand it into a generic folio.
4. **Editorial restraint.** Typography and whitespace do the work. One accent, deliberate motion. Loud is the enemy.
5. **Skim-first, reward depth.** Readable in 30 seconds on mobile; rewards the recruiter who scrolls and the founder who opens a deal drawer.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Maintain contrast on the warm-dark palette (verify muted text and gold/sand accents against near-black). Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`: gate reveal animations, the flow stepper, and map pulses behind the query so reduced-motion users get a static, fully-legible page. Keyboard-navigable interactive elements (deal drawers, nav, map hotspots) with visible focus states.
